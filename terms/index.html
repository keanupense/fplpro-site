1. About FPL Pro

FPL Pro is an iOS application that helps Fantasy Premier League managers make better transfer and squad selection decisions. The App reads your FPL team data from the official Fantasy Premier League API and applies statistical models to generate transfer recommendations, captain picks, expected-points projections, fixture difficulty ratings, and squad selection advice.

FPL Pro is developed and maintained by a solo developer based in Berlin, Germany.

3. Description of Service

FPL Pro provides the following features:

  • My Team — view your current FPL squad, player prices, and team value
  • Transfer Advice — algorithmic transfer recommendations based on expected points, fixture difficulty, price trends, and ownership data
  • Selection Advice — squad selection and captain recommendations for upcoming gameweeks
  • Build Team — squad-building tools and fixture roadmap analysis
  • FDR Matrix — Fixture Difficulty Rating matrix across multiple gameweeks
  • Player Watchlist — save and track individual players and teams
  • Sell Value Overrides — manually adjust player sell values to reflect your actual FPL account

All advice and recommendations are generated algorithmically from publicly available FPL data. They represent statistical suggestions only and do not constitute a guarantee of any particular outcome.
