Under the hood

The model,
explained simply.

FPL Pro doesn't just look at this week. It plans ahead.

1

Connects your FPL squad

Link your FPL Entry ID in 10 seconds. The app loads your squad, bank balance, and transfer history from the official FPL API.

2

Calculates expected points per player, per fixture

The xPts engine uses per-90 rates, fixture difficulty, minutes expectation, clean sheet probability, and bonus likelihood — not just form or price.

3

Runs beam search across your planning horizon

Instead of asking "who's best this week," it asks what the optimal squad state looks like in 4–6 gameweeks — and which transfers get you there.

4

Ranks plans and explains each one in plain language

Every suggestion comes with reasoning: points added, bank freed, captain improvements, blank-week coverage. No black box, no vibes.
